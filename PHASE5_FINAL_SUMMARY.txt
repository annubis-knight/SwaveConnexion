RESPONSIVE DESIGN APPROACH
==========================

Mobile-First Strategy:
  - Base styles apply to mobile (320px+)
  - md: prefix for tablet+ (768px+)
  - lg: prefix for desktop (1024px+)

Examples:
  - Header: hidden (mobile) → md:flex (tablet+)
  - Footer: grid-cols-1 (mobile) → md:grid-cols-3 (tablet+)
  - Hero: py-20 (mobile) → lg:py-32 (desktop)
  - Hero: text-5xl (mobile) → lg:text-6xl (desktop)
